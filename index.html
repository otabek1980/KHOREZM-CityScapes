Khorezm is one of the ancient and historical regions of Uzbekistan,
located in the lower reaches of the Amu Darya River.

About Khorezm

Khorezm is considered the cradle of ancient civilizations. As early as the first millennium BCE, the region had large cities, irrigation systems, and a highly developed culture. Renowned scholars like Al-Biruni and Al-Khwarizmi were born in this land and made immense contributions to science and knowledge.

The heart of Khorezm is the city of Khiva, known for its unique architectural monuments. Its old inner city, Ichan-Kala, is still preserved behind ancient walls and has been listed as a UNESCO World Heritage Site. Tourists from all over the world are drawn to its historical atmosphere and timeless beauty.

The people of Khorezm have long been known for their hospitality, oral traditions, and distinctive customs. Celebrations, weddings, and traditional ceremonies are held with great enthusiasm.
